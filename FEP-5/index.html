COCONUT MILK

Sweet iced Coffee with
Coconut Milk

Total time

10 minutes

Level

10 minutes

Budget

Under 2$

Sweet Iced Coffee with coconut milk is easy to make and stores well in the fridge.
Make a large batch and save yourself a few trips to the coffee shop

Ingredients

3 tablespoon Espresso coffee powder

1 cup off the boil water

1 cup Coconut milk

3 tablespoon Espresso coffee powder

Instructions

  1. 1. Boil some water and let sit for 1-2 minutes.
  2. 2. Put the coffee grounds into a cafetière and pour in the water.
  3. 3. Let the coffee steep for 5 minutes then slowly press down the plunger on your cafetière.
  4. 4. Pour the coffee into a jug, allow to cool then chill for several hours
  5. 5. Whisk in the coconut milk and condensed milk and serve over plenty of ice
  6. 6. This coffee can be stored in the fridge for up to 5 days. Shake or stir again before serving
